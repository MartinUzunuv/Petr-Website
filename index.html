About

I believe that my experience in the field of Power Industry, Energy and Investments, my abilities and qualifications make me a reliable partner and a trusted advisor. In last 10 years I am deeply involved in the Europian Energy Sector. In my career development I went through stock exchange and OTC dealing and administration of state-held shares in Czech companies owned by National Property Fund. I had the chance to participate in privatisation of 60% equity interest in Komerční banka, a.s. through public competition; privatisation of 66 % equity interest in ČSOB, a.s. through public competition; privatisation of 52% equity interest in Česká spořitelna, a.s. through direct sale; preparation for privatisation of 97% equity interest in Transgas, a.s. My managerial experience and skills in Finance and Energy was the reason to be appointed as Managing Director of MERO Pipeline, GmbH Vohburg a.d. Donau, Germany, daughter company of MERO ČR, a.s., which owns and operates the Druzba and IKL crude oil pipelines. I was involved in the implementation of a new SCADA control and safety system, preparation of crude oil reverse pumping from the Czech Republic to Germany. I had the chance to play a significant role in Hutní montáže, a.s., one of the biggest European companies in the area of construction and installation of structural steel, bridges and process equipment as Financial Manager and Vice-Chairman of the Board of Directors. In last 5 years I am deeply involved in the Bulgarian Energy Sector as COO and Vice-chairman of BoD ČEZ Bulgaria EAD, Vice-chairman of BoD of ČEZ Elektro Bulgaria AD, Vice-chairman of BoD of TEC Varna EAD and Chairman of BoD of Free Energy Project Oreshets EAD – 4.8 MWp solar power plant. I am eager to face new opportunities, help giving a birth of new ideas, bring life to new corporate or investment projects in the years to come!
